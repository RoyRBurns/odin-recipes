Ravioli

Description

This Baked Ravioli is an easy, dinner for children that takes just 10 minutes to prepare! Cheesy ravioli is tossed with pasta sauce and Italian sausage, topped with parmesan and mozzarella and baked to a dilicious finish.

Plain pasta isn't always going to cut it, especially with picky children! Baked Ravioli is quick and easy, all while being a tasty spin on the classics!
